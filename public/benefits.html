Benefits of Using MSpace

MSpace creates a trusted marketplace that helps you find artisans near you in Nigeria, book artisans online, and hire artisans in Nigeria with confidence. Below is a deeper look at the specific benefits for each user type.

For Customers

Faster, safer hiring

MSpace removes guesswork when hiring artisans. You can compare profiles quickly, read reviews, and verify professionals before you book. This is especially helpful when searching for an electrician near me Nigeria or a plumber near me Nigeria.

Because booking and messaging happen in-app, your communication is documented and you receive progress updates in one place.

  • Access nearby artisans in minutes.
  • See transparent ratings and reviews.
  • Chat before you book to confirm price and scope.
  • Track bookings and updates from start to finish.
  • Build a list of trusted professionals for future needs.
Result: faster service, lower risk, and more confidence in your choice.
For Artisans

More visibility, more jobs

Artisans gain a digital storefront that highlights skills, certifications, and past work. This makes it easier to win customer trust and grow faster.

MSpace helps you manage bookings and build a reputation through reviews that improve your ranking and visibility.

  • Showcase skills and experience professionally.
  • Receive job requests from nearby customers.
  • Build trust with verified profiles.
  • Increase repeat customers through reviews.
  • Save time with centralized communication.
Result: higher demand, better reputation, and steady growth.
For Businesses

Reach clients at scale

Business accounts allow teams to present a structured profile with multiple service categories, coverage areas, and company branding.

This creates a professional presence that attracts larger clients and long-term opportunities.

  • Showcase services to a wider audience.
  • List coverage area and team size.
  • Manage bookings and communication in one place.
  • Build consistent trust with reviews and ratings.
  • Create a professional digital reputation.
Result: more visibility, stronger brand trust, and bigger opportunities.
