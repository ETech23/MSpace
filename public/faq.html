Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for customers, artisans, and businesses who want to find artisans near me Nigeria and book artisans online. If you need more details, contact our support team.

What is MSpace?

MSpace is a marketplace that connects customers with skilled artisans and businesses. It helps users discover services, communicate in-app, and book reliably.

Who can use the app?

Anyone can register as a customer, artisan, or business. Each account type has tools tailored to their needs.

How do I know a provider is trustworthy?

Profiles show ratings, reviews, and verification status. You can also chat before booking to confirm details.

How does booking work?

After selecting a provider, you can chat to confirm the job, then book in-app and receive status updates.

Can I run a business on MSpace?

Yes. Business accounts can showcase teams, list multiple services, and define coverage areas.

How are reviews handled?

After a service is completed, customers can leave reviews and ratings to help others decide.

Is location required?

Location helps match customers with nearby professionals. You can control permissions in your device settings.

How do disputes work?

Disputes can be raised in-app and reviewed by admins with evidence and messages for resolution.
