MSpace Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

MSpace ("we", "our", "us") provides a marketplace that connects customers and artisans. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Account details: name, email, phone number, profile photo.
  • Location data: approximate or precise location when enabled.
  • Usage data: app interactions, crash and diagnostics data.
  • Messages and content: chats, reports, reviews, and uploaded files.
  • Device data: push notification token, device type.

How We Use Data

  • Provide core marketplace features.
  • Enable communication, bookings, and notifications.
  • Improve safety, detect abuse, and enforce policies.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Data Sharing

We may share limited data with service providers needed for app operation (for example hosting, authentication, notifications, and analytics). We do not sell personal data.

Data Retention

We retain data as long as needed for product operation, legal obligations, dispute handling, and fraud prevention. Retention periods may vary by data type.

Your Choices

  • Manage profile visibility from in-app privacy settings.
  • Request account deletion from in-app settings or via our Account Deletion page.
  • Manage permissions (location, camera, notifications, microphone) from device settings.
