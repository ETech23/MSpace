Account Deletion

Last updated: February 21, 2026

You can request deletion of your MSpace account at any time. Requests are processed in accordance with our privacy policy.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the MSpace app.
  2. Go to Settings → Account.
  3. Select “Delete Account” and follow the prompts.

Data Retention

We may retain certain data for legal, safety, or fraud prevention reasons as required by law.
