MSpace Community Guidelines

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Be Respectful

No harassment, hate speech, threats, or discrimination.

Be Honest

No scams, impersonation, fake listings, or fraudulent claims.

Keep Content Appropriate

No explicit sexual content, graphic violence, or illegal content.

Use Messaging Responsibly

No spam, repeated unsolicited messages, or abusive language.

Safety Tools

  • Use in-app report for users, jobs, and messages.
  • Use in-app block controls for unwanted interactions.

Enforcement

Violations may lead to warnings, content removal, temporary restrictions, or permanent account removal.

Contact

Safety reports: support@mspace.app
