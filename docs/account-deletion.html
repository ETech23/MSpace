Delete Your MSpace Account

Last updated: February 21, 2026

In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  1. Open MSpace
  2. Go to Profile -> Settings -> Delete Account
  3. Confirm the deletion request

Deletion Request by Email

If you cannot access the app, email support@mspace.app with:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your phone number (if available)
  • Subject: "MSpace Account Deletion Request"

What Gets Deleted

We delete or anonymize account-linked personal profile data, subject to legal and fraud-prevention retention requirements.

What May Be Retained

  • Records required by law
  • Fraud, abuse, and dispute-resolution logs for limited retention periods
